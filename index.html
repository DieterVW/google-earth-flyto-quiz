FRUIT, A GOOGLE EARTH FLYTO QUIZ

Demo of my quiz template for (the new) Google Earth for Chrome and Android (june 2017)

Try the demo. It's tasty!

  1. Download the KML file.
  2. Surf to http://g.co/earth (in Google Chrome or in the new Google Earth Android app).
  3. Click on 'My Places' in the sidebar.
  4. Click 'Import KML file' and open the KML file you just saved (to your download-folder).

Build your own flyto-quiz for free!

Just adapt my template at Github .
